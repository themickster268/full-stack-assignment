Welcome to the NI Broadband Report 2020


The data collected is based on the Ofcom Connected Nations report of May 2020.

For each postcode, the percentage of premises able to recieve a certain range of download speed, for each download speed range (0-2Mb/s, 2-5Mb/s, 5-10Mb/s, 10-30Mb/s, 30-300Mb/s, over 300Mb/s).

The report also includes a ranking of Postcodes in terms of percentage of premises unable to receive the Universial Standard Obligation (10Mb/s download and 1Mb/s upload).

The lower the percentage, the higher the number of premises able to receive USO broadband.

Users are welcome to leave a comment about braodband provision at their postcode.
